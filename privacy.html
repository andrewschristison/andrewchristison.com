Privacy

This is a personal website. It does not collect personal data.

No cookies. No analytics. No trackers. No forms. No email capture.

Sound preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers whether you muted it. That data never leaves your browser.

External links go out to retencity.com, ask-k.retencity.com, pondlog.co, cropgraph.com, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Those sites have their own privacy policies. They are not me.

The Open Graph image and meta tags exist so links shared on LinkedIn or X show a thumbnail. The image and meta description travel with the URL; they don't report back.

The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may keep standard server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) per their privacy policy.
